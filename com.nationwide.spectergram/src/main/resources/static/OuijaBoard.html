Ouija Board


Instructions:

Step 1 - Form a semi circle around the mouse with all participants facing the screen and each
participant must have 3 fingers from each hand on the mouse.

Step 2 - Assign one person as the medium and have the medium greet all the spirits in the room.

Step 3 - The medium must ask a question then ask a spirit to come forward and guide the mouse to
reveal an answer. If the mouse hovers over a button on the board for more than 1 second the value
of the button will become part of the answer.

Step 4 - After each question is answered the medium must thank the spirit for the answer and say
goodbye to the spirit, then bring the mouse to the goodbye button for 1 second.

Note : The medium can be changed after each question but no one other than the medium must speak.
